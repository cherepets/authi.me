Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we handle to provide our service to you.

Our principles

  • We do not collect personal information. We don't ask for, store, or have access to your name, email address, or any other personal details. Your identity is not known to us.
  • Your data is yours. The data you create and use within our application is for your eyes only.
  • You hold the key. We use end-to-end encryption, and the keys to decrypt your data are stored exclusively on your devices (client-side). We cannot decrypt your information.

How we use data

The encrypted sync data is used for the sole purpose of providing the application's core functionality, such as keeping your information up-to-date across your devices. We do not analyze, aggregate, or sell any user data.

How we secure data

To enable features like syncing your information across multiple devices, we need to store some data on our servers. However, this data is handled with the highest level of security:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted on your device before it's sent to our servers, ensuring it remains private during transit and while stored.
  • Client-Side Keys: The decryption keys required to read this data are generated and stored only on your local device(s). We do not have access to these keys, and therefore, we cannot view, access, or share your content. If you lose your keys, even we cannot help you recover the data.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the application or by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
